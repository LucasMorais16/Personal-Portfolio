About

Lemniscata is a first-person horror game currently in development in Unreal Engine since January 2025. I conceived this project in 2024, driven by my passion for the horror genre and a desire to create something uniquely my own. After refining the concept, I enlisted three collaborators to help bring the vision to life.

I am responsible for overall project production, programming core mechanics, UX/UI logic, animation, and game design. I manage the development pipeline using Jira, assigning tasks and coordinating team efforts.
